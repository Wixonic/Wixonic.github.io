Complex

undefinedThe complex is a set of constructions designed initially as shelters for humans and certain animals that sought refuge from the Great Cold. After the Derangement, it became a haven for the Wixies. The complex consists of 12 Silos connected by Tubes.

History

The complex was originally created by the Founders to protect humans, as well as some of the fauna and flora, from the AEON reactor explosion. The exact number of initial Silos in the complex remains unknown, but traces of Silo No. 17 have been found in Relic No. 1177. Today, only Silos No. 1, No. 2, No. 3, No. 5, No. 6, No. 7, No. 8, No. 9, No. 10, and No. 12 are operational and/or accessible. Silo No. 4 is no longer functional, and No. 11 is inaccessible following Containment Procedure 11-0.

Structure

!Simplified layout of the complex Each Silo is entirely independent, but collaboration between Silos is permitted under the Second Pact, making the transport of technologies, documents, and other goods frequent in order to reduce technological disparities between the Silos. Although cables and pipes are visible in some layouts extracted from declassified relics, the origin of the power supply and water supply remains unknown.

Legends and Beliefs

Due to isolation after the Derangement, the Wixies began to speculate about what might lie beyond the walls of the complex. This led to the emergence of various legends:
  • Belief of Level 1, a belief in a theoretical Level 1 within each Silo.
  • Fear of Humans, a legend referring to theoretical oppression and domination exercised by humans prior to the Derangement.
